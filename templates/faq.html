FAQ

What is this?
HowPoor.RU is similar to an EVE killboard, but instead of tracking killmails it tracks wallets. Both corporation and personal wallets are supported, and all information is made available on the site.
How often does HowPoor.RU update?
Character wallets update every 2 minutes.
Corp wallets update every 5 minutes.
Journal transactions update every hour.
This means that sometimes the wallet amount may not be in sync with your journal transactions. If there is a difference between the wallet amount and the amount field on the latest transaction, the journal transactions are probably a bit behind.
Can I remove my data?
No, once your data is public there is no removing it.
However, you can remove your ESI scopes to prevent new wallet data from being added. Visit the account page to manage your ESI scopes.
Why are there wallet transactions for my character? I've never logged in!
Even if you have never logged in to HowPoor.RU, if you are involved in a transaction with someone who is on the site that transaction will show on your page.
Some information will be missing, like your wallet amount at the time of the transaction, and of course not all transactions that you are involved in will be displayed.
Why do some transactions just have numbers for the 'context' field?
This happens when the context number can't be turned into something that HowPoor.RU can deal with. This is the case for things like citadels IDs, contract IDs, and other things that would require additional ESI scopes to handle.
I tried searching for (x) and got no results!
HowPoor.RU populates its database dynamically. If HowPoor.RU hasn't encountered what you are searching for in a wallet transaction yet, it won't appear in search results.
